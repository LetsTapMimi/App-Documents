Summary: Buzz Catcher™ is designed to be privacy-respecting by default. We do not sell personal data. Most information you enter is stored locally on your device unless you explicitly enable features that require data transmission.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

  • Preferences and optional configuration inputs used for calculations.

B. Subscription Information

If you subscribe, payments are processed by Apple. We do not receive your payment card information. We may receive limited subscription status information (such as active or expired) to unlock features.

C. Diagnostics

We may collect crash logs and basic performance diagnostics to improve app stability. This may include device model and operating system version.

D. Support Communications

If you contact support, we collect the information you provide (such as your email address and message).

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and operate app functionality.
  • To personalize your experience based on your settings.
  • To improve performance, reliability, and features.
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information with service providers that help operate the app (such as analytics or crash reporting services), only as necessary to perform their services.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users or the public.

You can manage or delete your logged information within the app. If you need assistance, contact us using the information below.
